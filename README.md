**FriendlyTodos** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by FriendlyTodos.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application, FriendlyTodos, and its associated services. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide the social task management experience. The types of information we collect include:

### A. Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your email address and username. If you sign up using Google, we receive your basic profile information from Google.
- **User Content:** We collect the content you create, including your to-do lists, task details, goals (yearly and five-year), and completion status.
- **Social Interactions:** We collect data regarding your interactions, such as friend connections, "pokes" sent/received, and task suggestions.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We collect device identifiers (specifically APNs/FCM tokens) to send you push notifications.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with our Service to improve performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Provide the Service:** Manage your to-do lists, authenticate your identity, and enable social features.
- **Communication:** Send you push notifications for friend requests, received suggestions, and "pokes" from friends.
- **Personalization:** Generate weekly summaries comparing your productivity with your friends.
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage trends to fix bugs and improve app performance.

## 3. Sharing of Your Information

### A. With Other Users (Social Features)
FriendlyTodos is a social application.
- **Friends:** Users you explicitly accept as "Friends" can view your **public** to-do lists, your username, and your productivity statistics.
- **Private Tasks:** You have the option to mark specific tasks as "Private" within the app. Private tasks are **never** visible to your friends.
- **Interactions:** Friends can see when you complete public tasks and can send you "pokes" or suggestions.

### B. Service Providers
We share information with third-party vendors that perform services on our behalf:
- **Firebase (Google):** We use Firebase for Authentication and Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications).
- **Hosting Providers:** Our backend API is hosted on third-party servers which store your account and task data.

### C. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or using the delete account feature within the app (if available).

## 5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. We use secure authentication methods (Firebase Auth) and standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) for data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
